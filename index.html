About the project

A new way to visualize the MoMA art collection

I have always struggled to connect art movements throughout the 19th and 20th centuries to their specific time periods. Additionally, I have always wondered which artists overlapped where, where in time did this occur and how might've they influced each other? While I can't answer the latter with data visualization what I can do is develop a system where we can begin to visually understand where these overlaps occur, and ultimately present a different approach to learning about art movements through the 19th and 20th centuries.

Generate Product Development - Website

As a UX / UI Designer at Generate Product Development, I, along with a team of Designers of Developers, were tasked with completely redesigning the company website from the ground up. ArtistLaunch

A project I completed during my time at Generate Product Development, ArtistLaunch aims to unify artists and playlist curators on applications like Spotify, Apple Music and Pandora. Through this relationship formed by ArtistLaunch, where curators browse and bid on artist's songs, negotiations can be made between two parties, enabling artists that might not otherwise have a wide reach or much visiblity to increase their exposure. I was the Design Lead on this project, where myself and 4 other designers worked this project from the ground up, right from workflows and mood-boards to a fully functional prototype in a hi-fi state.

App Development - Brigham Education Institute

During my time at Brigham and Woman's Hospital and Harvard Medical School, I spent a large portion of my time as the head UX and UI Designer on the BEI app development team.
